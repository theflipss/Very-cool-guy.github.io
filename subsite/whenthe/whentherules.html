WELCOME TO WHENTHE RULES!!!

As of May 2026

  1. Be Funny Your post should make sense and contain an actual joke. Do not make posts whose only joke is a copypasta or a comically long caption. Do not make spam or nonsensical posts. Do not make posts that just describe a life event.
  2. Be Original Be original; if you see a hilarious joke here don't try to recreate the meme with a few changes, that's how you get repetitive memes like the 2000th skinwalker meme we've seen. We won't list every type of punchline and meme we see, it should be obvious if you're blatantly copying a joke.
  3. No Meta (dumb rule) If the purpose of your post is to commentate on a subreddit, don't post it. Same goes if your post is about whenthe drama or some form of complaining. Your post must stand on its own outside of this subreddit or else it is likely going to be removed. If all your post boils down to is "They suck and do annoying thing", it is inappropriate. Posts in the format of “r/pisspoopers users when I piss and poop” or posts complaining about the subreddit, for example, are not allowed. Lore posts are ok.
  4. Post Criteria Your post should be formatted as a gif with a black on white/white on black caption at the top. You can also use bolded fonts. Make sure it's centered and readable. Note that some of the criteria here can be broken if it's for the purpose of a joke.
  5. Don't Repost Anything posted in the past 3 months or anything in the top 30 posts of our subreddit can't be reposted. This is done to keep things fresh for new and old members and is simply a good practice. You may use or crosspost memes that follow our criteria from other subreddits or Discord servers, but before doing so make sure it hasn't been posted already by searching through the subreddit. If your posts are OC, this shouldn't pertain to you.
  6. Don't be a degenerate Do not make posts explicitly discussing and/or describing gross topics (fetishes, sex, gore, bodily fluids, etc). “What would my parents say if they knew I posted this?” is a good guideline to follow in regards to this. This also includes any form of bigotry, in general or directly aimed at other users.
  7. About Politics You may make posts about political topics as long as it's tasteful, funny, and light-hearted. Do not make posts to simply push an agenda (“waffle supporters getting sent to hell because waffles suck and I hate them”). Do not make posts with the intent to enrage and bait interaction. Be civil even when discussions get heated.
  8. No news posts Posts that simply declare a news event without an actual joke are banned. Instead, try and format it as a joke in a way that fits the subreddit. For example, instead of writing “Supercell just announced that they will use AI in their games! [Screaming Kevin gif]”), write “When I'm playing a Supercell game in 2026 and notice that all new cards have 6 fingers and a mysterious piss color filter”. Check out r/whennews if you want to see and make classic newsposts.
  9. Don't blatantly be an asshole Just don’t be a dick honestly, keep your opinions to yourself and treat everyone the same.
